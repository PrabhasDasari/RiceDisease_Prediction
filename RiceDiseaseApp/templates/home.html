Rice is one of the most essential crops worldwide, serving as a staple food for a significant portion of the global population. However, rice cultivation faces numerous challenges, including the threat of various diseases that can significantly impact crop yield and quality. To address this issue, our project focuses on leveraging deep learning techniques for the automatic detection and classification of diseases in rice plants. Utilizing deep learning algorithms, particularly convolutional neural networks (CNNs), our project aims to develop a robust and efficient system capable of accurately identifying common diseases affecting rice plants. These diseases include but are not limited to blast, bacterial blight, sheath blight, rice tungro disease, and brown spot.
