In this project for rice disease detection, we collect a dataset of rice leaf images containing healthy and diseased examples of various rice plant diseases. Preprocess the images by resizing and normalizing them. by employing a pre-trained VGG16 model, removing the final classification layers specific to the original dataset it was trained on.Add new classification layers on top of the pre-trained VGG16 model, suited to the number of rice disease classes the project aims to identify.Train the model on the prepared rice disease image dataset.Evaluate the model's performance on a separate test dataset to assess its accuracy in classifying different rice diseases.
