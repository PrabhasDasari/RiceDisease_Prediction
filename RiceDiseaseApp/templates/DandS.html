Leaf Blast
Symptoms
Oval or elliptical, grayish-white lesions with green edges on leaves
Lesions may turn brown and enlarge, eventually killing the leaf
Stunting of plants if infected early
Reduced number of tillers
Leaf Blight
Symptoms
Large, irregular, brown or yellow patches on leaves
Blighted leaves may appear scorched or dried
Can cause entire leaves to die back
May be accompanied by yellowing or mottling of leaves
Brown Spot
Symptoms
Small, round, brown spots on leaves and leaf sheaths
Spots may enlarge and merge, causing significant leaf damage
Severely infected leaves may turn yellow and die
Plants may be stunted if infected early
Tungro
Symptoms
Orange or yellow discoloration of leaves, starting from the tips
Stunted growth of plants
Leaves may become brittle and upright
Sterile spikelets (no grains produced)
